03 · The solution

Four mathematical engines — not another chatbot.

Hidden Markov Models

Recognizes latent states — normal, anxious, crisis — from daily signals.

3 states, transition matrix A, emissions bj

Bayesian updating

Converts symptoms into risk probability using population prior.

Kalman filter

Forecasts tomorrow's anxiety state from today's observations.

Viterbi algorithm

Finds the most probable path through states across the week.
